Introduction

purpose of this Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) is to outline the acceptable practices and behavior when using the IHA089-LABS. By accessing or using the labs, you agree to abide by the terms set forth in this policy.

General Usage Guidelines

  • Authorized Use: The IHA089-LABS are intended solely for local, offline use on a single PC and are designed to be hosted only on localhost (i.e., your local machine).
  • Prohibited Activities: The following actions are strictly prohibited:
    • Using the labs for illegal activities, including but not limited to hacking, unauthorized access, or data theft.
    • Attempting to access external servers or networks using the labs.
    • Distributing or hosting the labs on the internet without prior written permission from IHA089.
    • Modifying or redistributing the labs for commercial purposes without explicit permission.

Limitations

  • The labs are for educational and testing purposes only. They should not be used for any malicious activities or to disrupt other users or systems.
  • The labs must only be run in a local environment (localhost) and must not be exposed to external networks.
