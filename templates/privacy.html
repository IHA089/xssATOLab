Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when using the IHA089-LABS. Since the labs are designed to be used only locally and on a single PC, your privacy is of minimal concern, and we take no steps to collect personal data.

Information Collection

  • No Personal Data Collected: The IHA089-LABS do not collect any personal information. The labs are designed to work entirely on your local machine, with no data transmitted externally.
  • Local Data: Any data generated during the use of the labs, such as logs or configuration files, remains on your machine and is not shared or sent to any external servers.

How Information is Used

Any local data, such as logs or system settings, is solely for the purpose of running the labs on your machine. There is no data collection for marketing, analysis, or any other purposes.

Data Retention

  • Local Storage: Data generated by the labs is stored only locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or retain any data remotely.
  • User Control: You have full control over any data generated by the labs and can delete it at any time.

Security Measures

  • No External Access: The labs are intended for local use only. Your data does not leave your machine, and no external access is possible.
  • User Responsibility: You are responsible for securing your local machine and any data that might be generated or stored during the use of the labs.

No Sharing or Selling of Data

We do not share, sell, or lease any data generated by the use of the labs. All data remains on your local machine.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

If any changes are made to this Privacy Policy, they will be updated in the official documentation. Since the labs do not collect or transmit data externally, no notification method is required.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@iha089.org.in.
